Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I create an account?
A: Click Sign Up on the app and fill in your personal details. Verify your email to activate your account.
Q2: How can I track my order?
A: After placing an order, go to My Orders to see the order status and tracking details.
Q3: What payment methods are accepted?
A: We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and selected regional payment methods.
Q4: Can I cancel my order?
A: Orders can be canceled before they are shipped. Go to your orders page and select Cancel if available.
