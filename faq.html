Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I create an account?
A: You can create an account by clicking the Sign Up button on the home screen and filling in your details.
Q2: How can I track my order?
A: After placing an order, go to My Orders in your profile. You will see the order status and tracking information.
Q3: What payment methods are available?
A: We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other regional payment methods.
Q4: Can I return a product?
A: Yes, returns are allowed according to our Return Policy. You can initiate a return through your account.
